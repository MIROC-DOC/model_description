Basic Settings

Here we present the basic setup of the model.

Coordinate System

座標系は、基本的に、経度λ\lambdaλ、緯度φ\varphiφ、正規化気圧η\etaη (定義は後述) を用い、それぞれは直交するとして扱う。ただし、地中の鉛直座標はzzzを用いる。

Longitude is discretized at equal intervals MODULE:[ASETL].

λi=2πi−1I      i=1,…I−1 \lambda_i = 2 \pi \frac{i-1}{I} \;\;\; i = 1, \ldots I-1 λi​=2πIi−1​i=1,…I−1

The latitude is the Gauss latitude φj\varphi_jφj​ described in Mechanics, and it is derived from MODULE:[ASETL], the Gauss-Legendre integral formula. This is the zero point of the Legendre polynomial of order J with μ=sin⁡φ\mu = \sin \varphiμ=sinφ as the argument, MODULE:[GAUSS].

If J is large, we can approximate

φj=π(12−j−1/2J)      j=1,…J−1 \varphi_j = \pi ( \frac{1}{2}- \frac{j-1/2}{J} ) \;\;\; j = 1, \ldots J-1 φj​=π(21​−Jj−1/2​)j=1,…J−1

Usually, the grid spacing of longitude and latitude is taken to be approximately equal to J=I/2J = I/2J=I/2. This is based on the triangular truncation of the spectral method.

Each predictor is entirely defined on a grid of (λi,φj,σk)(\lambda_i, \varphi_j, \sigma_k)(λi​,φj​,σk​) or (λi,φj,zl)(\lambda_i, \varphi_j, z_l)(λi​,φj​,zl​). (The underground level, zlz_lzl​, is described in the section on physical processes.)

In the time direction, the predictive equations are discretized at evenly spaced Δt\Delta tΔt and time integration is performed. However, if the stability of the time integration may be impaired, the Δt\Delta tΔt may change.
