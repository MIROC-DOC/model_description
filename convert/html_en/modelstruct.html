Special note on the program.

There are several modules with multiple entries using the ENTRY statement. Its main purpose is the local storage of data. For example, in the case of the module W2G above, the variables PNM and DPNM are It is stored as a local variable in this module, Commonly used in W2G, G2W and SPSTUP.
