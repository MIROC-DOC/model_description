Time integration and time filtering with leap frog

The leap frog is used as a time integration scheme for advection terms and so on. The backward difference of 2Δt is used for the horizontal diffusion term. In addition, the pseudo p surface correction of the diffusion term and the frictional heat term by horizontal diffusion are treated as a correction and becomes the forward difference in 2Δt. The physical process section (ℱλ, ℱφ, Q, Sq) is , I still use the forward differential of 2Δt. (However, we treat the calculation of the time varying term of vertical diffusion as a backward difference. See the chapter on physical processes for details.)
